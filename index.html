Facts about dogs

The dog is one of the two most ubiquitous and most popular domestic animals in the world (the cat is the other). For more than 12,000 years it has lived with humans as a hunting companion, protector, object of scorn or adoration, and friend.

Quick facts.

  • Dogs have a sense of time. It's been proven that they know the difference between an hour and five. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk time
  • Your dog is as smart as a two-year old. Ever wondered why small children seem to have a bond with dogs, it could be because they speak the same language roughly 250 words and gestures just to approximate.
